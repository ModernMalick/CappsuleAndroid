DESCRIPTION 
Relevant outfit ideas at your fingertips !
Cappsule is a wardrobe organizer for minimalists and capsule wardrobe enthusiasts.
It lets you save your clothes and suggests outfits based on conditions which you control such as their availability or the weather.
Dressing has never been this easy !
